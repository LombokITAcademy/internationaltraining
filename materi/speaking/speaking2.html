Speaking 2: Emma and Lisa

Emma and Lisa are discussing where to go for lunch. Emma suggests trying the new Italian restaurant nearby. Lisa thinks it’s a good idea but wonders if it’s expensive. Emma heard that the prices are reasonable and the food is delicious. Lisa agrees and suggests inviting their friend, Sarah, to join them. They decide to check the menu online before going. Both are excited to try something new and enjoy lunch together.

Quiz: Emma and Lisa
