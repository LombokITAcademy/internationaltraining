Reading 1: Tom the Soccer Player

Tom loves to play soccer. Every morning, he wakes up early and goes to the park to practice with his team. One day, his coach announced a big match against another team. Tom and his friends practiced hard for a week. On the day of the match, they played with all their energy and won! Everyone was very happy and celebrated together.
